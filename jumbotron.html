Jumbotron

A jumbotron is a large call-to-action box element that is used to call attention to specific areas of a web page such as a registering an email account or signing up to a newsletter distributed via email.

Introduction

This is an example of a jumbotron being used to display a "Insert email address" cue.

Register your email address

Want to keep up to date with our latest news and information?

Enter your email below to register.

Usage

Contained Width Jumbotron

<div class="container">
    <div class="jumbotron">
        <h1>Register your email address</h1>
        <p>Want to keep up to date with our latest news and information?</p>
        <p>Enter your email below to register.</p>
        <input type="email" placeholder="Your email address">
        <input type="submit" class="btn secondary filled" value="Register!!">
    </div>
</div>

Jumbotron in Full Width

If you want your jumbotron to take up the full width, remove the .container class.
